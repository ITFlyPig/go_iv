GoIV privacy policy


The application GoIV does not collect and transmit any personal information. It does not sell any information to 3rd parties.
The only information gathered is anonymized app-usage used to solve crash-bugs and develop the app features.

This anonymized reporting can be turned off in settings, but the crash-reports auto-generated for all google play apps are still
sent. 

The application collects and transmits the following information:

Anonymized application stats using Fabric. (How long the app is open, which parts of the app is used)
Crashreports. (Crashlog, does not contain any personal information, may contain data such as what device and android OS you have.)
